Whatsapp News

WhatsApp has become the second most popular social network on the planet, surpassed only by Facebook's 2.5 billion active users.

WHY DO YOU NEED WHATSAPP?

WhatsApp boasts over 1.5 billion active users worldwide, making it one of the most popular apps across numerous platforms, including iOS and Android. Despite these impressive numbers, users naturally worry about their privacy. It’s likely because WhatsApp is owned by Facebook, infamous for its security issues.

With WhatsApp, you don't have to pay any fees because it uses your iPhone's internet connection to let you message and call friends and family and there are no subscription fees to use WhatsApp.

FEATURES

Quickly connect with your contacts

Your address book is used to quickly and easily connect you with your contacts who have WhatsApp so there’s no need to ad hard-to-remember usernames. Send free messages to your friends and family. WhatsApp uses your mobile phone's Internet connection to send messages to avoid SMS charges.

Group chat

Keep in touch with the group of people you care about most, such as your family or colleagues. With group chats, you can share messages, photos, and videos with up to 256 people at once. You can also name your group, mute it, customize notifications, and more.

Calls and video chats

With the calls, you can talk to your friends and family for free, even if they are in another country. And with free video calling, you can have face-to-face conversations when voice or text isn't enough. WhatsApp calls and video calls use your phone's Internet connection instead of your wireless plan's voice minutes, so you don't have to worry about expensive calling charges.

Automatic security

Some of your most personal moments are shared through WhatsApp, which is why we developed end-to-end encryption in the latest versions of our application. With end-to-end encryption, your messages and calls are protected so that only the people you communicate with can read or hear them without anyone else, not even WhatsApp, being able to do so.

Better experience

Sometimes, your voice says it all. At the touch of a button, you can record a voice message to say hello or tell a story.

SECURITY

Speak freely

WhatsApp's Calling feature allows you to talk to your friends and family, even if they are in other countries. Like messages, WhatsApp calls are also end-to-end encrypted so that neither WhatsApp nor third parties can hear them.

Your messages belong to you

Your messages should always be in your hands. That is why, once they are delivered, WhatsApp does not keep a record of your messages on its servers. Also, with end-to-end encryption, neither WhatsApp nor third parties can read them.

Check it out

WhatsApp allows you to check whether the calls you make and the messages you send are encrypted from end to end. Simply view the indicator on your contact or group information screen.

Every day, our users send billions of stickers through WhatsApp to express what they think and feel without having to write a single word. Since we launched the stickers 18 months ago, they have become one of the most popular and fastest growing forms of communication in our application.

Dark Mode
